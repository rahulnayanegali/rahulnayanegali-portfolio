# My Portfolio Migration to Blog-Native

I decided to rebuild my portfolio as a blog-first site.

## Why

Previous site — now archived looked more of an amateur frontend dev trying to showcase his skills to have a breakthrough in the market whereas here I am in the year of 2026 running toward a new breakthrough not just as frontend engineer but as a frontend specialist and a backend/ai-driven/cloud/devops/prompt-engineer generalist.

## What's Scaring Me

- Sheer power of ai-cli tools
- Identity as a frontend engineer being shaken

## What I'm Doing

Deep down I’m welcoming this upheaval uncomfortably because we as a human beings are meant to unleash our creativity and to bring a new creation from our pristine mind and soon we accept the fact our job is not gonna remain the same in a year only makes the self adoption possible rather than lagging with our self-built arrogance of our little knowledge house. 

## What's Next

I don’t know how I should be adopting to this change and the advice on the internet is overwhelming. I’ve decided I’ll sleep over it to only allow my subconscious to understand this and show me a path ahead as inspired by Rich Hickey Hammock-Driven Development.

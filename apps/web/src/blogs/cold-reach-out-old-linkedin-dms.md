I was thinking how to cold reach out this week since I had exhausted my free Linkedin premium  in-mail credits.

I had this worry for over two nights and on Monday morning while I was about to start, my mind flashed an idea, why not reach out to my existing network from my Linkedin DMs who have some context about me if not they can find scrolling up right?

cool, thats what I did.

The recruiters are working on new roles and are looking for new candidates to fill asap. They do not have to spend time analysing your skills  and I don’t have to stop my cold reach out just because I exhausted my free in-mail credits.

So What does this tell me?

I think when you’re genuinely striving to perform a certain action ,not just worrying about it, your brain does heavy lifting for you about *the how*. Monday morning without overthink, I just showed up and the idea popped up. Old Dms. People whom I am not a stranger. New roles, fresh candidate hunt, I just have to sneak in.

*You don’t always need to go wider. Sometimes “the how“ is already in your inbox.*
